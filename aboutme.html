general summary

i go by qtPy online, and im a software engineer who specialises in tooling, pipeline development, VFX, games, reverse engineering, cryptography, and more

my goal is to continue to contribute to the FOSS movement and to commit to a 100% focus on cyber-independence. your information is NOT my information, and it never should be. you have the right to privacy, and to protection, no strings attached

i also strongly advocate for social issues, protecting civil rights, and disestablishmentarianism - the state should not have the power it does, and i am a staunch believer that it should be challenged, checked, and balanced consistently

TOGETHER we *CAN* fight for a better future
